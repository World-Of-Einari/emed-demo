TOPIC: Wegovy (Semaglutide) for Weight Loss

Wegovy is a brand name for semaglutide, a GLP-1 receptor agonist approved for chronic weight management. It is one of the two main medications prescribed through the eMed Weight Management Programme in the UK.

WHAT IS WEGOVY:
Wegovy contains semaglutide, which mimics the GLP-1 hormone naturally produced by the body after eating. It works by slowing gastric emptying, reducing appetite, and regulating blood sugar levels. Patients typically feel full more quickly, experience fewer cravings, and consume fewer calories without feeling restricted.

HOW IT IS ADMINISTERED:
Wegovy is a self-injectable medication supplied in a pre-filled pen. Injections are taken once weekly, subcutaneously — meaning just under the skin, typically in the abdomen, thigh, or upper arm. eMed provides supervised injection support through weekly check-ins with a healthcare technician.

DOSING SCHEDULE:
Wegovy follows a gradual dose escalation schedule to minimise side effects:
- Weeks 1–4: 0.25mg once weekly
- Weeks 5–8: 0.5mg once weekly
- Weeks 9–12: 1mg once weekly
- Weeks 13–16: 1.7mg once weekly
- Week 17 onwards: 2.4mg once weekly (maintenance dose)

The escalation schedule may be adjusted by your clinician based on tolerability and response.

CLINICAL EVIDENCE:
In the STEP 1 clinical trial published in the New England Journal of Medicine in 2021, participants taking semaglutide 2.4mg alongside lifestyle intervention lost an average of 14.9% of their body weight over 68 weeks, compared to 2.4% in the placebo group.

Wegovy has also received FDA approval for reducing the risk of major cardiovascular events — including heart attack and stroke — in adults with cardiovascular disease who are also overweight or obese. This makes it the first weight loss medication approved for this specific purpose.

STORAGE AND HANDLING:
Wegovy pens should be stored in the refrigerator between 2°C and 8°C. They can be kept at room temperature (below 30°C) for up to 28 days if needed. Pens should never be frozen. Used pens must be disposed of safely in a sharps bin.

IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTE:
Wegovy is a prescription-only medication in the UK and can only be legally obtained with a prescription from a licensed clinician. Patients should not purchase semaglutide from unregulated sources including social media, beauty salons, or websites that do not require a prior clinical consultation. Counterfeit products have been reported and pose serious health risks.
