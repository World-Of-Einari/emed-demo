TOPIC: Pricing, Subscription, and Programme Costs

The eMed Weight Management Programme operates as a monthly subscription. Pricing varies depending on the medication prescribed, the dose, and any periodic changes to the cost of the medication itself.

HOW PRICING WORKS:
The price quoted at checkout is the amount charged periodically (typically monthly). This price is determined at the time of your clinical assessment and reflects:
- The specific GLP-1 medication prescribed (Wegovy or Mounjaro)
- Your prescribed dosage
- The cost of the medication at the time of prescription

eMed reserves the right to adjust pricing in response to changes in medication cost price — for example, if the wholesale price of a medication rises or falls. Any price changes are communicated to patients in advance and discussed before being applied.

WHAT THE SUBSCRIPTION INCLUDES:
The monthly subscription covers:
- Your GLP-1 medication delivered to your door
- Weekly check-ins with an eMed healthcare technician
- Monthly clinical reviews with a licensed clinician
- 24/7 side effect support
- Access to the progress tracking portal
- Ongoing prescription management

Some elements of the programme — such as virtual physiotherapy and mental health support — may be included or available as part of a comprehensive package depending on the plan selected.

PAYMENT OPTIONS:
eMed may offer the option to pay for several months upfront, potentially at a discounted rate. The availability and terms of multi-month payments may vary and eMed reserves the right to revoke discounts if not yet applied and paid for.

CANCELLATION:
The programme is structured as a monthly subscription, meaning patients are not locked into a long-term contract. You can stop the programme at any point. eMed recommends following the programme for at least six months to see significant and lasting results, but the monthly structure allows patients to take it one month at a time.

BLOOD TEST COSTS:
The initial blood test may be included in the programme cost or charged separately depending on the plan. eMed does not accept external lab results, so the eMed-provided kit is required for all new patients.

NO EQUITY OR HIDDEN FEES:
eMed does not charge additional fees for clinical reviews, side effect support calls, or prescription adjustments within the programme scope. The subscription model is designed to be transparent about what is included each month.

IS IT AVAILABLE ON THE NHS:
GLP-1 medications such as Wegovy and Mounjaro are available on the NHS in some circumstances, but access is currently limited and subject to strict clinical criteria and waiting lists. eMed operates as a private provider, offering faster access with a structured support programme. Patients interested in NHS routes should speak to their GP.

INSURANCE:
eMed is a private service and is not covered by standard UK health insurance. Patients should check with their individual insurer if they believe their policy may cover weight management programmes.
