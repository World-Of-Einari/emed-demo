TOPIC: eMed Weight Management Programme Overview

eMed is a digital health company offering a comprehensive, clinician-led weight management programme built around GLP-1 medications. The programme combines prescription medication with ongoing clinical support, lifestyle coaching, and regular check-ins to help patients achieve sustainable weight loss.

Unlike standalone prescription services, eMed provides a structured end-to-end programme. This includes an initial eligibility assessment, blood testing, a clinician review, a personalised treatment plan, and continuous monitoring throughout the patient's journey. The programme is available as a monthly subscription and can be started or paused at any time.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE PROGRAMME:
- Eligibility questionnaire and health profile assessment
- At-home blood test kit and live-proctored blood collection
- Clinician review of lab results and medical history
- GLP-1 prescription (if eligible) delivered to your door
- Weekly check-ins with an eMed healthcare technician
- Monthly clinical reviews with a licensed clinician
- 24/7 side effect support with no appointments needed
- Access to virtual physiotherapy sessions
- Nurse-led mental health support
- Behavioural support programme
- Progress tracking portal

HOW THE PROGRAMME WORKS:
1. Complete your health profile and eligibility questionnaire online.
2. Receive an at-home blood test kit. A live virtual proctor guides you through the collection process.
3. A licensed clinician reviews your lab results and medical history to determine your eligibility and the most appropriate medication.
4. If eligible, your GLP-1 medication is dispatched directly to your home.
5. You begin weekly check-ins with an eMed healthcare technician to monitor progress and support medication administration.
6. Monthly clinical reviews ensure your prescription remains appropriate and dosage is adjusted as needed.

WHO IS THE PROGRAMME FOR:
The eMed programme is designed for adults aged 18 to 75 who are struggling with weight management. It is particularly suited to individuals who have tried diet and exercise without achieving lasting results, or who have weight-related health conditions such as type 2 diabetes, high blood pressure, or elevated cardiovascular risk.

WHY EMED DIFFERS FROM OTHER PROVIDERS:
eMed emphasises safety, adherence, and long-term outcomes over quick fixes. Patients are not simply handed a prescription — they are enrolled in a monitored programme that addresses the root causes of weight gain, including nutrition, physical activity, sleep, and stress. The company does not offer unregulated or compounded medications, and all prescriptions are issued by licensed clinicians following a full clinical assessment.
