TOPIC: Mounjaro (Tirzepatide) for Weight Loss

Mounjaro is a brand name for tirzepatide, a dual GIP and GLP-1 receptor agonist approved in the UK for chronic weight management. It is available through the eMed Weight Management Programme and is considered one of the most effective weight loss medications currently available.

WHAT IS MOUNJARO:
Unlike semaglutide (Wegovy), which targets only the GLP-1 receptor, tirzepatide targets two receptors: GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) and GIP (glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide). This dual action is believed to produce greater appetite suppression and more significant weight loss than single-receptor agonists. It works by slowing digestion, reducing hunger signals to the brain, and improving insulin sensitivity.

HOW IT IS ADMINISTERED:
Mounjaro is a self-injectable medication in a pre-filled pen, taken once weekly. It is injected subcutaneously — under the skin — in the abdomen, thigh, or upper arm. eMed provides weekly supervised check-ins to support patients with injection technique and monitor progress.

DOSING SCHEDULE:
Mounjaro follows a dose escalation protocol:
- Weeks 1–4: 2.5mg once weekly
- Weeks 5–8: 5mg once weekly
- Weeks 9–12: 7.5mg once weekly (if tolerated)
- Weeks 13–16: 10mg once weekly (if tolerated)
- Weeks 17–20: 12.5mg once weekly (if tolerated)
- Week 21 onwards: 15mg once weekly (maximum dose, if tolerated)

The dose escalation is guided by your clinician and can be paused or slowed if side effects are significant.

CLINICAL EVIDENCE:
Tirzepatide has demonstrated exceptional results in clinical trials. In the SURMOUNT-1 trial, participants taking the highest dose (15mg) achieved an average weight loss of approximately 22.5% of body weight over 72 weeks. This represents a step change in weight loss outcomes compared to earlier GLP-1 medications.

COMPARING WEGOVY AND MOUNJARO:
Both medications are effective for weight loss. Mounjaro's dual mechanism generally produces greater average weight loss, but individual responses vary. The clinician reviewing your case will recommend the most appropriate medication based on your health profile, medical history, and treatment goals.

STORAGE:
Mounjaro pens should be refrigerated between 2°C and 8°C. They can be stored at room temperature (below 30°C) for up to 21 days. Do not freeze. Dispose of used pens in a sharps bin.

REGULATORY STATUS IN THE UK:
Mounjaro (tirzepatide) has been approved by the MHRA for use in the UK for weight management. It is a prescription-only medication and must be obtained through a licensed clinician following a clinical assessment.
